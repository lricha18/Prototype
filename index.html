Star Catcher

  • High Concept: Catch as many stars before you fall off the screen.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players do not need experience of playing games to play this one.
  • Genre: This game is all about getting a higher score each time you play. There is no winning just doing a little bit better each time.
  • Game play: This game include a player that can move left and right and may jump if they are on the ground. The player includes an animation for the left and right movements. The core mechanics are the jumping and moving left to right. When the left arrow key is pressed, the player will move to the left. When the right arrow key is pressed, the player will move to the right. When they player is resting on the ground, the up arrow key may be pressed to make the player jump. The game camera is also moving to the right causing the player to run as they catch stars. The game gets progressively harder as the player gets more points by speeding up the camera's movement.
  • Prototype goal: This prototype is evaluating arrow key presses, object collisions, world bound collisions, and playing sounds.
  • Features: This game has off camera detection for the player. Each frame the game checks if the player has left the camera view and if they have the game is ended. Camera movement that gets progressively faster will make the player run faster to keep up.
  • Setting/Premise: This game takes place at the edge of the sea on a beach where there are many falling stars that can be caught for points. The further along you run on the beach, the faster you must run.
  • Story: The girl here finds herself on a beach where there are stars that are falling and she gains points by catching these stars for some reason.
  • Target Audience: This game targets an audience of children and adults of all ages.
  • Play time: Between 10 seconds and 5 minutes depending on the skill of the player.
  • Strategies: Get the highest score possible by catching stars without falling off the screen. A typical strategy is to always keep the right arrow key down. Additionally, making the player jump to catch the stars will make it easier to catch the stars.
