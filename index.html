Dungeon Escape

  • High Concept: The player is trapped in an alternate universe on the planet Earth-Z where they have been locked away in a dungeon. The player must use his wit and physical ability to escape from his prison and return to his home universe.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players may need some experience with puzzle solving to advance in this game.
  • Genre: This game will be a platforming game with many different levels.
  • Game play: The player will able to run left and right, jump, shoot bullets, and have super charged jumps. The player will use their bullets to shoot different targets that are on walls or enemies in their path. The player will also be able to charge their jump by crouching and then jumping to get double height on their jumps.
  • Prototype goal: This prototype is to show the basics that can be implemented further: Anti-gravity, Super jumps, shooting with the mouse.
  • Features: The player will be able to charge their jumps by crouching then jumping. The player will also be able to shoot enemies or targets to clear or open paths to advance to the end of each level.
  • Setting/Premise: This world is an alternate version of Earth where there are enchanted skeletons and prison keepers trying to keep the player from escaping. The player is from our universe and is very confused by the alternate universe that he got locked away in. The game will involve super natural occurrences that will not be normal to the player.
  • Story: This game takes place in a dungeon that the player has been locked up in. The player finds a gun and an instruction sheet in their cell. The player must use this and their skill to escape the dungeon. There will be many different types of enemies such as the many prison keepers and the enchanted skeletons that will try to keep the player from escaping.
  • Target Audience: This game targets all players ages 6-30.
  • Play time: The current level can take between 1-10 minutes based on skill of the player. The full game will have many levels to prolong the play time.
  • Strategies: The strategy of this game is to find the exit without dying. You have a gun and super jumps and you must use those to get out.
